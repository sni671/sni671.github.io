VTOL UAV

A 3D printed tricopter-style VTOL aircraft designed, built and flight tested as part of an ongoing personal UAV development project.

Project Overview

This project was developed to explore the design of efficient vertical takeoff and landing aircraft while maintaining the advantages of fixed-wing flight. The aircraft uses two front tilt motors and a rear fixed motor, allowing it to transition between hover and forward flight. The design combines aerodynamic analysis, CAD modelling, 3D printing, electronics integration and flight testing into a complete aircraft development process.

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of iterative design. Flight testing highlighted the difference between theoretical performance and real-world behaviour, leading to multiple refinements in aerodynamic design, structure, control systems, and integration. The development process involved continuous cycles of design, testing, analysis and improvement.
